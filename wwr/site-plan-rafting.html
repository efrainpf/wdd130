Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to promote teamwork, confidence and self-improvement through the practice of rafting.

Audience

Who are the target customers? We focus on young people between 20 and 30 years old who are studying or working in environments that have a lot of pressure and stress. What do they want? With our planned activities they can release stress, increase their confidence and create memorable memories for a lifetime. How will my audience usually access my site? It will be developed so that they can access it from their mobile devices with an internet connection, all young people currently have a cell phone and internet access.
